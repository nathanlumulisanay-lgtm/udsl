# Comparison: ChatGPT vs Gemini (UDSL Mode)

---

## 1. ChatGPT
- Strong alignment with YAML structure.
- Clear section headers.
- Reasoning complete but occasionally compressed.

## 2. Gemini
- Sometimes reorders minor sub-points unless explicitly constrained.
- Good tone control.
- Reasoning needs explicit Toulmin cues.

---

## Summary
Gemini is more flexible and sometimes creative with section boundaries.  
ChatGPT is stricter.  
UDSL standardizes both into predictable, stable long-form output.
